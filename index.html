Our Mission

CREATE club seeks to inspire, nurture and support future entrepreneurs through:

Skill Building

An effective entrepreneur is a well-rounded entrepreneur. CREATE Club offers purposeful activities and experiences targeted and developing various skills, from basic 3-D modeling to brainstorming effectively in a group.

Guest Speakers

Learning for those with expertise is another experience that CREATE Club brings to its members. These events are based around open-discussion, allowing for an insightful and personalized interaction between the speaker and the attendees.

Hands-On Activities

These activities give members a chance to get their hands dirty. With events like basic welding, these are not your typical entrepreneurial experiences, but knowledge of them allows for a better understanding of what goes on “behind the scenes” when developing a product or service.
