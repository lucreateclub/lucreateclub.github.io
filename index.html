Our Mission

Skill Building

An effective entrepreneur is a well-rounded entrepreneur. CREATE Club offers purposeful activities and experiences targeted and developing various skills, from basic 3-D modeling to brainstorming effectively in a group.

Guest Speakers

Learning from those with expertise is another experience that CREATE Club brings to its members. These events are based around open-discussion, allowing for an insightful and personalized interaction between the speaker and the attendees.

Hands-On Activities

CREATE members will get their hands dirty. With events like welding, these are not your typical entrepreneurial experiences; however, this allows for a better understanding of what goes on “behind the scenes” when developing a product or service.

So what's LehighHacks?

LehighHacks is a hackathon - an event where developers of all skill levels gather together to learn, pursue new projects, and generally have an awesome time. While the word "hack" has such a negative connotation these days, we strive to bring it a new meaning: the creation of exciting and innovative products in unprecedented ways.

What if I can't code?

This is the perfect place for you. Hackathons are first and foremost a place to learn about computer science. The best way to learn how to code is by doing it - especially with other people. LehighHacks will have a great atmosphere with mentors walking around the entire event to assist when needed.

What can I win?

We are working with our partners to get some awesome prizes for LehighHacks. We'll announce a final prize list as we get closer to the event.

How do I sign up?

Sign up at LehighHacks.com! You won't want to miss the event. We're really excited and you should be to!
